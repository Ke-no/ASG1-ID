About Us

In 2017, a Japanese rock duo was formed named Yorushika.
The band includes a Vocaloid producer, N-buna and a vocalist, Suis.
They are recognized for their juxtaposition of passionate and upbeat production,
and heavy lyrical content fused with instrumentation. The band ideas often come from works of literature, human emotion as well as love.

Please check us out! - Yorushika
